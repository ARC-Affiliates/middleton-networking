What Should You Verify Before Joining a Networking Group?

Verify the group's registration, insurance, member retention rate, and category-exclusivity policy before paying any annual fee.

Credentials to Confirm

  • Florida business registration — searchable via Sunbiz (dos.fl.gov/sunbiz).
  • General liability insurance — minimum $1M for groups hosting in-person events.
  • Written category-exclusivity policy — prevents two competitors paying for the same seat.
  • Published member retention rate — healthy groups retain 65%+ year-over-year.
  • Tax status disclosure — 501(c)(6) chambers vs. for-profit referral networks have different protections.

Pre-Join Verification Checklist

  1. Visit two meetings as a guest before paying.
  2. Ask three current members what their 12-month ROI looks like.
  3. Confirm your category isn't already filled.
  4. Read the cancellation and refund policy in writing.
  5. Verify the organizer's Sunbiz registration.
  6. Request the group's referral-tracking system or template.
  7. Confirm event venue addresses and parking near Middleton.
  8. Compare total annual cost (dues + meals + retreats) to projected referrals.
