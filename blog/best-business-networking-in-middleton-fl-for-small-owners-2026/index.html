How Does the Joining Process Work in 2026?

The joining process is the sequence of steps from first contact to active membership.

Most directories and groups follow a 4–6 step onboarding that takes 7–21 days from inquiry to active listing.

  1. Step 1: Inquiry — Submit a contact form or call the group. Expect a response within 1–3 business days.
  2. Step 2: Guest visit or demo — Attend a meeting or review a sample directory profile.
  3. Step 3: Application — Provide business license, insurance, and category preference.

What Mistakes Should You Avoid?

Networking mistakes are the common errors that waste dues, time, and reputation.

The biggest mistake is paying for membership without committing to weekly attendance and follow-up.

Pre-join verification checklist

  1. Confirm the group is registered on Sunbiz.org.
  2. Attend two meetings as a guest before paying.
  3. Ask three current members about their referral volume.
  4. Read the refund and cancellation policy.
  5. Confirm category exclusivity in writing (for closed groups).
  6. Verify the directory shows up on Google for relevant local searches.
  7. Check that automatic renewal terms comply with Florida Statute 501.0117.
  8. Set a 6-month review date on your calendar.
